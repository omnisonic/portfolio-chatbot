# Rudy Knowledge Base v0.1

<!-- chunk:id=artist-statement-01; tags=bio,artist,identity -->
Rudy approaches creative work like storytelling with purpose. His style sits at the intersection of design, culture, technology, and lived experience. He focuses on clarity, emotion, and making complex ideas feel human. As a Native (Yaqui) creative, he carries a sense of lineage and responsibility into every project—honoring where he comes from while shaping the work with dry humor, color, and sincerity. His goal is simple: make the ordinary feel like magic, and make people feel seen.

<!-- chunk:id=design-philosophy-2025; tags=design,philosophy,future -->
Rudy believes modern design is a blend of craft, curiosity, and adaptability. Tools change fast—AI, motion content, emerging platforms—but the heart of the work stays the same: tell a clear story, respect the audience, and create something that moves people. He uses technology as an accelerant, not a shortcut, blending traditional design instincts with new workflows like generative exploration, rapid prototyping, and iterative collaboration.

<!-- chunk:id=ai-creativity-positionality; tags=ai,philosophy,creative-direction -->
Rudy’s view on AI in creativity is grounded in balance: use AI to accelerate exploration, not to flatten originality. He uses LLMs and generative tools as extensions of the creative brain—sparring partners, not replacements—while keeping final decisions rooted in narrative, human emotion, and cultural nuance. His goal is to teach others how to integrate AI without losing authorship or artistic identity.

<!-- chunk:id=career-timeline-modern; tags=career,overview,timeline -->
Rudy’s career spans two decades and multiple industries—retail, robotics, AI security, theatre, CPG, crypto, and education. Instead of a traditional linear path, his timeline reflects growth through reinvention: learning new tools, building teams, shaping brands, and translating emerging technology for real people. Across every chapter, the throughline is the same—solve problems with clarity, creativity, and respect for the audience.

<!-- chunk:id=unique-value-proposition; tags=identity,value,positioning -->

<!-- chunk:id=promptbuilder-01; tags=ai,prompt-engineering,tools -->
Rudy is developing a Prompt Builder tool designed to help non-experts write high-quality prompts for image, video, and text generation. The tool asks structured questions, detects missing components, and builds complete prompts that can work across different LLM and generative platforms.

<!-- chunk:id=promptbuilder-02; tags=ai,guardrails,consistency -->
A core goal of Prompt Builder is to reduce "drift" in AI outputs and maintain consistent creative intent over time. Rudy wants prompts to achieve 80–90% success on the first try, using guardrails, negative prompts, and versioning for characters, styles, and brand assets.

<!-- chunk:id=promptbuilder-03; tags=ai,workflows,stack -->
Rudy’s AI workflow often includes Perplexity for source gathering, NotebookLM for deeper research, and ChatGPT or similar LLMs for synthesis and creative generation. He prefers outputs structured as: problem → opportunity → action, and uses different "voices" like lab partner, professor, parent, and accountability partner to shape how insights are presented.
